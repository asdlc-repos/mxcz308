# Overview

An internal leave management application for WSO2 employees to request time off and for managers to review and approve those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — reviews and decides on leave requests from their team
- HR admin — maintains leave policies and balances

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request by choosing dates and a leave type.
- Managers see requests from their direct reports and approve or deny them.
- Employees view their current leave balance for each type of leave.
- HR admins set each employee's annual leave allocation.
- Everyone can see the status of their submitted requests.
